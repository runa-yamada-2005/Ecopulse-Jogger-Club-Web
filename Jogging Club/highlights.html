Celebrate Our Most Memorable Runs. Revisit What Moved Us.

From sunrise challenges to costume jog weeks, our club thrives not just on routine, but on rhythm-breaking moments that energize and unite. This section is your highlight reel — a vibrant gallery of our most unique, creative, and nature-loving events. 

"Whether you ran through morning mist, danced in rain, or jogged in pajamas — this is where those stories live on."

🎨 FEATURED PAST EVENTS

     
  Sunrise Challenge:  
     
  5:45 AM at Hillcrest Viewpoint – Dozens of sleepy-eyed joggers gathered under a lavender sky. Warm-up by candlelight. Jog in silence. Ended with warm tea and shared reflections.  
     
  Member Moment: "Jogging while the world was still asleep… I felt like I was borrowing time from the sky." – Mira, Architecture  
     
     
  Nature Run: Tree Whisper Edition  
     
  Guided by an urban botanist, we jogged the Lakeside Loop while learning the names, stories, and scents of native trees. Jog. Pause and Listen.  
     
   
     
     
  Themed Jog Week: "Jog Your Mood"  
     
  Each day, jog in color that matches your mood. Yellow for joy. Blue for calm. Red for power. We ended the week with a jog + paint day!  
     
  Gallery: Paint-streaked shoes, laughing faces, a trail of rainbow footprints.  
     
  Member Moment: "I’ve never smiled this much while running." – Rizal, Year 1 Psychology
