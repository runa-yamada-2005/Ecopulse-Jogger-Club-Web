CLUB INTRODUCTION

ABOUT US | WHY JOGGING? | AWARENESS

You don’t need to be fast. You don’t need experience. All you need is a pair of comfortable shoes, an open mind, and a desire to move.

The Ecopulse Jogger Club is open to all students — from first-timers to regular joggers looking for community. If you’re ready to improve your health, meet friendly faces, and explore nature in a meaningful way, you’re already halfway there.

OUR CLUB VALUES

We believe jogging is a gentle but powerful way to care for your health, calm your mind, and connect with your environment. At the heart of our club are these values:
  • Inclusivity: All fitness levels and backgrounds are welcome
  • Respect: For yourself, others, and the Earth
  • Mindfulness: Jogging is as much about noticing as it is about moving
  • Community: We support and uplift each other — no pressure, no judgment
  • Consistency Over Competition: Every step matters, no matter the pace
  • Environmental Stewardship: We clean as we jog and leave no trace
