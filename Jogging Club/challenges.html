MONTHLY JOG
CHALLENGE!

Looking for a little push to keep jogging consistently — or just want to try something new and fun with fellow members? Every month, the Jogging for Health & Nature Club hosts a themed challenge designed to keep your feet moving, your mind refreshed, and your connection to nature strong.

"Challenges are beginner-friendly and open to all members. You can participate solo or with a buddy!"

This Month’s Challenge:

June 2025 — “Nature Photo Run”

Theme: Combine jogging and photography

Your mission:

  • Go for 3 jogs this month in a natural setting (park, trail, riverside)
  • During each jog, take at least one photo of something you notice — a plant, tree, bird, shadow, sunrise, etc.
  • Share your favorite photo + 1–2 sentences about it using our ✨ submission form or in the group chat

Bonus: Featured photos will appear on the club’s homepage and social media!

Submission deadline: June 28, 2025

Reward: Digital “Nature Noticer” badge + shoutout

📩 How to Participate

  1. Check the new challenge posted at the start of each month
  2. Complete the simple task at your own pace
  3. Submit your entry via the Monthly Challenge Form or in the member chat
  4. Get featured — and inspired!

🎉 Why Join Monthly Challenges?

  • Build consistency without pressure
  • Try something new every few weeks
  • Stay connected to the club and yourself
  • Celebrate progress with peers
  • Discover new places, habits, and thoughts

🧢 What’s in it for You?

  • Recognition in our challenge wall
  • Digital achievement badges
  • The occasional surprise giveaway (club merch, eco gear, free event access)
  • The joy of showing up for yourself!
