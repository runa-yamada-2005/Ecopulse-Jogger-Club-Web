WELCOME!

Jogging doesn’t have to be fast or far. It can be gentle, refreshing, and deeply connected to the world around you.
At the Jogging for Health & Nature Club, we jog through shaded trails, quiet parks, and green corners of the city — noticing flowers, feeling the breeze, and building our health, together.
Whether you’re new to jogging or returning after a break, we welcome you to join a community that runs with purpose, joy, and care for the Earth.
