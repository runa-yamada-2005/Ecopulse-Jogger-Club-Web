MINDFUL JOGGING TIPS

  • Jog without headphones once a week and just listen to your surroundings
  • Try to notice one new thing on your regular route
  • Use your breath as your rhythm — inhale for 3 steps, exhale for 3
  • Carry a reusable bottle — stay hydrated sustainably!
 

If you're unsure which pace or plan fits you, talk to any of our club guides during meetups, or send us a message in the group chat. We’re here to help you jog with confidence, safely and joyfully.
