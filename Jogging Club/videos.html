Jogging transforms with the seasons, offering a unique atmosphere and rhythm throughout the year. This page is a celebration of that journey — from the fresh energy of spring to the golden calm of autumn, the brightness of summer to the quiet of winter. Through a series of video clips submitted by our members, you’ll experience how each season shapes the mood, scenery, and connection we feel while jogging.

These clips capture not just the paths we run, but the moments of peace, clarity, and joy found along the way.

SEASON!
In spring, the activity feels refreshing and hopeful — nature awakens, flowers bloom, and the scent of wet grass fills the air, making each step feel like a new beginning. Summer brings bright, energetic runs, often during sunrise or sunset. With long daylight hours and warm weather, joggers take to lakesides and shaded trails, staying cool and hydrated under the sun.

As autumn arrives, the atmosphere shifts — jogging paths are lined with falling leaves, and cool breezes create a comfortable, reflective pace. The golden-orange landscape becomes a favorite for nature-loving runners. Even winter has its quiet charm; despite fewer jogs during rainy days, misty mornings and crisp air offer a peaceful, meditative rhythm, making winter runs a time for calm and stillness.
