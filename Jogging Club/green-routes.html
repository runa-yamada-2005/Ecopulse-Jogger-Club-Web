Urban Green Routes

Kuala Lumpur is home to several urban parks and nature-rich jogging paths. These green routes not only promote physical fitness, but also provide mental relaxation through scenic views, fresh air, and surrounding biodiversity.

🌳 Perdana Botanical Gardens

This large public park offers a variety of jogging trails surrounded by lakes, trees, and gardens. It’s one of the most popular nature-friendly jogging areas in the city.



🌴 Taman Tasik Titiwangsa

A spacious park perfect for joggers, with paved tracks around a lake and lots of open greenery. Ideal for morning or sunset jogs.



These routes show how urban development and natural preservation can go hand in hand. Students are encouraged to explore these eco-friendly tracks and reflect on their connection with the environment.
