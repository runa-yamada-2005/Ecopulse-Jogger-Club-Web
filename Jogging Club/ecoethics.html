Respecting Nature While You Run

Jogging is not just a physical activity — it’s a chance to be a steward of nature. By being mindful of how we interact with our environment, we help preserve the beauty of our parks and trails for everyone.


🌿 Eco-Friendly Jogging Tips

  • 🗑 Don’t Litter: Always dispose of waste properly. Carry a small trash bag or use bins along your path.
  • 🛣 Stay on Trails: Stick to marked paths to protect plant life and reduce erosion.
  • 💧 Use Refillable Bottles: Avoid disposable plastics by bringing a reusable water bottle.
  • 🐦 Respect Wildlife: Observe animals quietly from a distance without feeding or disturbing them.
  • 🌳 Support Green Spaces: Volunteer for park clean-ups and conservation events when you can.
